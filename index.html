Mez Restaurant

In the heart of St Julians, Malta, MÉZ stands as an epitome of culinary excellence and is celebrated as the best seafood restaurant in the region. Mez is more than just a restaurant; it's an expedition into the enchanting world of Indian Coastal Cuisine, influenced by the captivating history of the Indian Ocean.

Naanbar Restaurant

Discover Naan Bar, your new favourite destination for modern Indian cuisine, right in the heart of Valletta on Old Bakery Street. Join us for a truly social dining experience where we present a contemporary twist on nostalgic dishes, honoring age-old traditions and techniques.

Chatterbox Restaurant

Nestled on the vibrant Islets Promenade in Buggiba, Malta, Chatterbox is renowned for its inviting ambiance, top-notch quality, and unbeatable value. This beloved Indian restaurant has consistently garnered rave reviews and a perfect five-star rating, making it the go-to dining spot in the area.

Calico Restaurant

Calico is designed for food enthusiasts, locals, and travelers who seek more than just a meal but a memorable culinary adventure. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, meeting for business, or simply enjoying a night out, Calico caters to those with a passion for global flavors, vibrant atmospheres, and elevated dining experiences.

Unforgettable Experiences at Araiya


Active Lifestyle

The mountains are calling, and they can barely wait. Get ready for an exhilarating time with a variety of adventurous activities you can choose from when you stay at our luxury adventure resort in Palampur.

Cultural Immersions

Delve into the vibrant culture of Palampur with our cultural immersions. Take part in traditional Tibetan pottery workshops, visit ancient temples, and hike through local villages.

Self Discovery
