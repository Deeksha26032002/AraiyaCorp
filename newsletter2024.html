Smart Technologies

Ensure greater guest satisfaction and operational efficiency. Our Guest Service Management platform empowers us to accomplish more with lesser time and effort. Prologic First provides cost-effective, comprehensive support, including mobile tracking, real-time updates, and efficient management of multiple services.

Hyper-personalization

Using data, analytics and AI creates highly personalised recommendations and customised loyalty programmes and maximises revenue. Experium by RepUp streamlines operations from booking to feedback, offering analytics on guest stays and sentiment.

Sustainability


Is an important focus at all stages of the hotel development. Sourcing local materials, local labour, reusing excavated materials, and using Energy Saving Devices (ESDs) lower our carbon footprint to the bare minimum. Natural resources such as light and air are optimised. Every Araiya hotel has its own reverse osmosis (RO) water plant and in-house bottling facility. We adopt reusable and recyclable items and practices in our daily operations and have eliminated plastic. Organic waste is recycled efficiently. We also promote a culture of conscious travel among our guests.

Inspiring Incentives

Araiya Palampur is cradled in the charming Kangra Valley, known for its landmark views of the Dauladhar mountains. The resort offers 25 luxurious suites, all-day multicuisine dining, rooftop bar, outdoor lawn areas, spa, heated pool, and fully equipped gym.

A world of adventure, discovery and solitude lie within and beyond Araiya’s gates. Adventure-seekers can find their rush with paragliding, hiking and walking tours. Solitude seekers can immerse themselves in the good vibes of McCleodganj’s monasteries and the surrounding temples, or practise Niksen (the Danish art of doing nothing) at a riverside picnic. The culturally inclined can discover the joys of pottery classes, cooking lessons, and rustic village walks.

Treks and tea garden walks. Sights and serenity. Lawn lunches and rooftop cocktails. Short stopovers or long stays. Whover you are, whatever you love, mesmerising moments at Araiya Palampur always welcome you.
