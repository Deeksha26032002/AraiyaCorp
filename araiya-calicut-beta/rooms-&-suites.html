Rooms & Suites

Our rooms and suites are situated around the tranquil expanse of the swimming pool. The exterior is designed keeping the traditional architecture of the Malabar coast in mind, while the interiors reflect European, Middle-Eastern or Oriental elegance. Furnished with character and charm, each room is embellished with cheerful textiles, art and artifacts.The villa-style accommodations are thoughtfully laid out to create a sense of privacy. Each room or suite leads out onto a private garden or patio with comfortable seating.
