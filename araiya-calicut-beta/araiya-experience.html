Araiya Experiences

The Malabar Coast is rich in heritage, both natural and cultural. Our stretch of the coast is situated at the delta of the Kadalundi River, where it flows into the Arabian Sea, to create lush wetlands, home to an abundance of marine biodiversity.

Ancient wellbeing to handloom weaving to martial arts, this stretch of India’s west coast is one of the best-kept secrets in the southern subcontinent. Calicut and it’s surroundings are famous for crafts, wellness and performing arts. Let’s explore!

History & Heritage

Far-flung folk, cultures, and influences have washed up on the shores of the Malabar Coast for centuries. Some never left - adding their own spice to the Malabar melting pot.

Take a stroll down Gujarathi Lane in Calicut and discover little-known facts about India’s Freedom Struggle. Discover the terracotta traditions of the Basel Mission. Learn more about the Calico weaves that made their way halfway across the world from these very shores. Visit local craftsmen and watch as they create beautiful woodwork and carvings with their magic touch. The Malabar coast is renown for it’s cultural heritage.

Picture Perfect

Malabar’s scenic allure only increases with time. Over the years, this stretch of the coast has inspired many people in their creative journey.

Whether it’s artists who are from other parts of the world, or those who call the Malabar coast home, tribal or urban, classic or contemporary, The Lalithkala Academy has become a home away from home for a variety of artists across different genres.

Are you a shutter-happy camera junky, or an experienced photographer? The natural beauty of the landscape, rich ethnic heritage, and intricate rituals of local life on the Malabar coast, are simply enchanting.

By The River

The Kadalundi River is a lifeline throughout the region. As it flows towards it’s delta with the Arabian Sea, it opens up into a series of lush mangroves and wetlands – a gorgeous, secret haven for several species of resident and migratory birds, as well as a variety of aquatic life.

Binoculars ready? It’s sheer paradise for bird lovers. Explore tiny fishing communities, plantations and hidden hamlets as you take a leisurely boat ride down the river. Cast a line into the water and see what you reel in. Take a stroll or meditate as the sun lingers over the horizon, casting a golden glow upon the river.

Cocoon In Your Comfort Zone

If you don’t feel like stepping beyond the boundaries of our resort, don’t worry. There’s plenty to do on property.

Enjoy a work out in the gym. Take a stroll or a bike ride on the estate.Unwind at the spa. Stretch at the yoga pavilion.

Experience the culture of Kerala with live performances by local artistes each evening. Discover Kerala’s coastal cuisine.

Kick back with your favourite book, or just chill out by the pool or in your private garden. Plug into your holiday playlist, and share the best “travelgram” insta-moments with your social media buddies, so that they can virtually relive your bliss!
