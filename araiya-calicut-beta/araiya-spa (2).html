The Araiya Spa is a sanctuary for your senses. Set a little away from the rest of the estate, the spa facility and yoga pavilion are located on the banks of the Kadalundi river, which lulls you into a restful state of tranquility in no time at all.

Experience an array of treatments and therapies from different parts of the world, customized by our therapists to revitalize you. From Swedish massages to Ayurveda, we draw on various techniques from the west, the east, and the Indian Subcontinent.
