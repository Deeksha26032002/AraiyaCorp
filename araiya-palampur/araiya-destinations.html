Araiya Palampur

A Norwood Heights Resort

Cosy, snowy, sun-kissed, old school, charming – these are some of the many adjectives that come to mind when you think of the mountains in Himachal Pradesh. Then again, these are also the ones you can describe Araiya Palampur with.

Nestled in the Kangra valley, this getaway in the tea capital of Northwest India is your escape from everything dull and dreary. With 25 suites to choose from, all-day dining, rooftop bar, outdoor lawn areas offering stunning views, we have your comfort and convenience taken care of. Looking for a relaxing experience? Try the heated pool, a session at the gym or the well-appointed spa.

Fancy the bracing outdoors? Head out for a tea garden walk or a private trek from the property. Allow the concierge to help you plan activities whether treks and leisurely walks in the valley or local sightseeing and tours.

At Araiya Palampur, we retain the charm and pace of a quaint Himachal town. You will only be delighted to discover it for yourself.

A Prabha Resort

SOUL BY ARAIYA PAITHALMALA

A Vihaara resort

Perched at a height, Soul by Araiya Paithalmala is a sliver of heaven on earth. Tucked away in a remote corner, on the border of Karnataka and Kerala, our Paithal hill resort is one of South India’s best-kept travel secrets.

For those who have a passion for nature and wildlife, Paithalmala is paradise. Stay with us at our resort in Paithalmala, and enjoy gorgeous hikes with stunning views, accompanied by birdsong and the sound of the wind stirring through the tall grass.

If you just want to kick back and relax, then indulge yourself in a blissful Ayurvedic treatment at the spa at our Paithalmala resort. Find a quiet spot in the garden for yoga, or meditate as the sky turns into spectacular shades of crimson at sunset.

Situated close to our resort at Paithalmala, the Brahmagiri Wildlife Sanctuary is full of elephants, lion-tailed macaques, barking deer and flying squirrels. Several rare species of birds, like the Malabar Trogon, can be found here as well so keep your camera and binoculars handy! For those who are spiritually inclined, legend has it that the Thirunellai Temple nearby was built by Lord Brahma himself. Trek up the steep slopes of Brahmagiri Hill, and pause to admire the beauty of the Irrupu and Chelavera waterfalls. Lull yourself into a blissful state as you immerse yourself in a world of nature and wellbeing.

Untouched. Unspoiled. Soul by Araiya Paithalmala is a sanctuary where you can let your body unwind, leave your mind to wander, and allow your spirit to run wild.

Our holistic spa and wellness resort near Mumbai serves as your own private sanctuary, where everything feels effortless, where one can simply be.

ARAIYA WATERFRONT, ALIBAUG

Serviced Apartments by Earthweavers

Situated in Dhokawade, a quaint neighbourhood that is a few minutes driving distance from the Mandwa pier, Araiya Waterfront Serviced Apartments, in Alibaug, by Earthweavers, is just across the water from South Mumbai. At thirteen stories tall, it is the first high-rise on this side of the bay — ideal for family holidays or corporate getaways!

Two bright and airy apartments on each floor offer a sense of privacy, with views of a serene waterbody. These 2100 square foot luxury service apartments in Alibaug have three bedrooms, a spacious living area, and a vast deck balcony. Well-designed bathrooms include separate walk-in showers, while the kitchenette is fully equipped. There is ample parking in a secured area, amid neatly landscaped grounds.

These leisure and corporate serviced apartments in Alibaug are fully furnished, optimized with the latest technology, so that you can move in at a moment’s notice. Your comfort is enhanced by the Araiya bed experience and consciously-sourced branded toiletries and amenities. A beautiful swimming pool, all-day dining bistro and bar, a lovely rooftop terrace, a gym, and an Araiya Spa, make it the perfect place for a family and friends weekend getaway. Whether you’re travelling on holiday or a corporate stay in Alibaug, this property promises to be your home away from home without any hassles.

Earthweavers integrates traditional techniques, materials and forms, and interprets them into a contemporary context, seamlessly blended with Araiya’s emphasis on personalized service and distinctive experiences. Together, we offer smart, convenient, and holistic hospitality, keeping the modern-day nomad in mind, to bring you the very best apartment hotel in Alibaug.
