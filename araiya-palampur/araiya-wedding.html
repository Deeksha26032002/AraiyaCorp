Weddings By Araiya Palampur

Click To Inquire

If you’re considering a destination wedding in India and wish to explore options beyond a beach setting, Araiya Palampur offers stunning mountain venues that provide the perfect backdrop for your special day. Escape the hustle and bustle of city life for a more intimate celebration with your family and friends.

Exchange vows nestled in the embrace of the mountains, surrounded by the beauty of nature. Our enchanting mountain wedding venues create a romantic atmosphere, ensuring your wedding day is truly unforgettable. Choose from a selection of exquisite venues in the picturesque hills of Palampur and make your wedding a day to cherish forever.

Nestled amidst the majestic charm of the Dhauladhar mountain ranges, Araiya Palampur is the perfect sanctuary for couples seeking intimate weddings that truly resonate with their love story. Our serene mountain venue offers an idyllic backdrop that combines breathtaking natural beauty with luxurious comfort, setting the stage for a celebration that is uniquely yours.

At Araiya Palampur, we understand that an intimate wedding is all about cherishing shared moments with your closest family and friends. Our dedicated team is committed to crafting a bespoke experience tailored to your desires, ensuring every detail reflects your vision. From enchanting outdoor ceremonies framed by stunning vistas to beautifully decorated indoor spaces, we create a warm and romantic atmosphere for your special day.

Our venue provides ample space to accommodate vibrant baraats, allowing for a lively and joyous celebration that showcases your traditions and culture. Indulge your guests with exquisite catering options featuring a delightful fusion of local and international cuisines, prepared to perfection and served with impeccable attention to detail. Our thoughtfully designed accommodations provide comfort and elegance, allowing your loved ones to unwind and revel in the enchanting surroundings.

Beyond the wedding festivities, we offer a diverse range of experiences for your guests to enjoy during their stay. Discover the breathtaking landscapes on guided nature walks, immerse yourself in rejuvenating wellness treatments, or engage in workshops that celebrate the rich culture of the region.

Choose Araiya Palampur for your intimate wedding celebration, where each moment is infused with love and joy, and your guests are treated

to an indulgent experience in the heart of nature's splendour. Let us help you create unforgettable memories that will last a lifetime.
