High Flyer - Paragliding At Bir Billing

Soar up high into the sky. Gaze down upon stunning vistas of the Dhauladhar mountain range below. Feel the rush! Go tandem paragliding with an experienced pilot who holds an international tandem paragliding licence. It is an exhilarating yet perfectly safe way to get a bird's eye view of the Himalayas.

Once you've come back to earth again, explore the Sherbling Monastery and the Baijnath Temple, which hold a deep spiritual significance in the local community. Enjoy a tasty snack at a local cafe before heading back to the Araiya Palampur.

Himalayan High - Hike To Houstan

Cool and refreshing, the early morning air is filled with the scent of pine needles and the twitter of birds. Hike up a gorgeous mountain trail towards Houstan, one of the many peaks that makes up the Dhauladhar Range. A Walk On The Wild Side - Garhi Hike

Spend a day outdoors and imbibe the stunning natural beauty of the Kangra Valley. Rooted in tradition, this village is made up entirely of quaint gaddi style homes that are inhabited by the local community. After working up an appetite, return to the Araiya Palampur for a delicious meal and a relaxing evening.

Tibetan Traditions - A Walking Tour Of Mcleodganj

Drive to the nearby town of McLeodganj, which is a 1.5 hour drive through the mountains from Palampur. This is one of the most important centres of Tibetan Buddhism in India. Visit the temple of the Dalai Lama and take a walk around the Kora. Make a wish as you spin the prayer wheel and place your prayer flags over the Kora. Finish off your visit with a trip to the local market, and a delicious meal of Tibetan treats at a local cafe. Head back through the mountains to the Araiya Palampur just in time for a lovely dinner.
