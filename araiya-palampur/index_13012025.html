          Frequently Asked Questions
          Q1.Where is Araiya Palampur located?
          Araiya Palampur is located in the beautiful hill station of Palampur in the Kangra Valley of Himachal Pradesh, India. The hotel is situated at Bundla Tea Estate Road, Palampur.
          Q2.What are the amenities offered?
          Araiya Palampur offers a range of amenities to ensure a comfortable stay for its guests. Some of the amenities provided include spacious and well-appointed rooms, a multi-cuisine restaurant, a bar, a spa and wellness center, a fitness center, a swimming pool, conference facilities, and free Wi-Fi access throughout the property.
          Q3.Is there a restaurant at Araiya?
          Yes, Araiya has one of the top restaurants in Palampur. The Dining Room where you can enjoy all day dining, offering a diverse range of options, from global gourmet dining to indulging in local Kangri cuisine. You can also grab a drink of your choice and relax at The Old Fox Bar.
          Q4.Does Araiya have a spa?
          Yes, Araiya Palampur has a spa and wellness center where guests can indulge in rejuvenating treatments and therapies. The spa offers a variety of services, including massages, body treatments, and facials, to help guests relax and unwind during their stay.
          Q5.Are there any recreational facilities?
          Araiya Palampur provides several recreational facilities to enhance the guest experience. The hotel features a temperature controlled/heated swimming pool where guests can take a refreshing dip. There is also a fitness center equipped with modern exercise equipment for those who wish to stay active during their stay.
          Q6.Which popular attractions and sightseeing in palampur are close to Araiya?
          Araiya Palampur offers a luxurious escape amidst expansive landscapes, located in the picturesque Kangra Valley, surrounded by natural beauty. Some nearby attractions include hiking, paragliding at Bir Billing, Dharamshala, and the famous McLeod Ganj, the residence of the Dalai Lama. Click Here and choose from a variety of experiences when you stay at our luxury resort in Palampur."
          Q7.Do you have meeting and event facilities?
          Yes, we have state-of-the-art meeting and event spaces, making our resort one of the top hotels in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh, an ideal destination for intimate weddings, conferences, and special events.
          Q8.Do your rooms come with a view?
          Many of our rooms feature picturesque views, including scenic landscapes and magestic snowcapped mountains, making Araiya one of the best place to stay in Palampur.
          Q9.Do you provide transportation?
          Yes, Araiya Palampur offers airport transportation for guests. We recommend calling ahead to confirm details.
