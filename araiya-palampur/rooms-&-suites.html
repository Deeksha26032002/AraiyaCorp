Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the rooms at Araiya Palampur stand out?

At Araiya Palampur, our luxury hotel rooms are designed to offer the perfect blend of comfort, modern amenities, and scenic beauty. Each room, from the cozy Deluxe Rooms to the expansive Executive Suites, provides stunning views of the Dhauladhar range and a serene environment that allows you to unwind and relax. Whether you are here for business or leisure, our rooms are designed to cater to every need, offering premium amenities like Wi-Fi, room service, and luxurious bathroom toiletries.

What amenities are included in the luxury suites at Araiya Palampur?

Our luxury suites at Araiya Palampur are designed to provide the ultimate comfort. Each suite includes modern amenities such as Wi-Fi Internet access, air conditioning, satellite television, room service, and bathroom toiletries. The Deluxe Suites also feature a separate living space, while the Duplex Suite offers additional space for families with three bedrooms and stunning mountain views.

Are the Executive Suites suitable for families?

Yes, the Executive Suites at Araiya Palampur are perfect for families. These spacious suites feature two bedrooms and a separate living room, ensuring that everyone has enough space to relax. With amenities such as Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and room service, the Executive Suites offer the ideal balance of comfort and luxury for both families and business travelers.

Can I get a mountain view from the rooms at Araiya Palampur?

Absolutely! Every room at Araiya Palampur, including our Deluxe Rooms, Deluxe Suites, and Executive Suites, offers breathtaking views of the Dhauladhar range. Whether you are relaxing in the comfort of your room or dining in our restaurants, you will enjoy the beauty of the surrounding mountains and lush greenery that define our tranquil resort.

Are there any family-friendly options for accommodation?

Yes, Araiya Palampur offers excellent family-friendly accommodation. Our Duplex Suites are especially designed for families, featuring three spacious bedrooms, a living room, and a private balcony. Additionally, our Executive Suites also provide ample space with two bedrooms and a separate living area, making them a great choice for families looking for comfort and convenience.

What is the difference between the Deluxe Rooms and Deluxe Suites?

The Deluxe Rooms at Araiya Palampur offer a cozy 250 sq. ft. space designed for relaxation with all the essential amenities like Wi-Fi, satellite television, and a comfortable bed with scenic views of the Dhauladhar range. The Deluxe Suites, on the other hand, are more spacious, with 590 sq. ft. of living space that includes a separate living area and bedroom. The Deluxe Suites offer additional comfort and privacy, making them ideal for guests seeking more space or longer stays.

How can I book a stay at Araiya Palampur?

Booking a stay at Araiya Palampur is simple. You can book directly on our website to take advantage of exclusive offers, or contact our reservations team for assistance. We also offer personalized booking options to help you choose the perfect room or suite for your stay.

Do you provide any special offers for extended stays?

Yes, we offer special packages for extended stays at Araiya Palampur, providing great value and discounts for guests who wish to experience the serene beauty of Palampur for a longer period. Contact our reservations team for more details on our long-term stay offers and how you can make the most of your time with us.
