Dining

Dining at Araiya Palampur - A Gourmet Experience in the Hills


Indulge in a gourmet dining experience like no other when you stay at Araiya Palampur. With an expansive menu and a variety of unique dining experiences, we offer more than just food , we offer an experience. Whether you are savoring authentic local cuisine or enjoying

an international spread, every meal at Araiya Palampur is designed to thrill your taste buds and excite your senses. From fine dining with a view to relaxed outdoor picnics, we ensure that every moment you spend with us is truly special.

The Dining Room

All-Day Gourmet Dining

At The Dining Room, every meal is a journey of flavors. Start your day with a sumptuous buffet breakfast or choose from our à la carte menu for lunch and dinner. Indulge in a mix of global gourmet dining or relish the authentic Kangri fare, prepared with the freshest ingredients and utmost care.

Designed to provide an immersive fine dining experience, The Dining Room offers a luxurious setting where you can enjoy your meal in style. Whether you are having the first meal of the day, a leisurely lunch, or a cozy dinner after an exciting day, we offer a range of options to suit your mood.

Enjoy our exquisite service and delicious food in a setting that befits the luxurious experience you are promised at Araiya Palampur.

The Old Fox

A Tranquil Lounge Experience

Step into The Old Fox Bar, a place where time moves at the pace you desire. Set in a tranquil lounge overlooking the pool and the majestic mountains, this luxury bar offers the perfect atmosphere to unwind with a drink of your choice.

The bars luxurious interiors and sophisticated furnishings make it a spot that guests rave about. Whether you are enjoying a laid-back afternoon or a beautiful evening, The Old Fox Bar is where you will want to be.

Take in the splendid views, enjoy a refreshing drink, and relax with a good book in hand. With a drink in one hand and the mountains in view, this bar provides a truly gourmet dining experience under the open sky.

The Terrace

Outdoor Dining with Magnificent Views

Dining at The Terrace is a feast for the senses. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Dhauladhar range and Palampurs breathtaking landscape, this outdoor dining area offers the perfect setting for a private dinner or a gathering with friends and family. Whether you are enjoying a luxury dinner under the stars or sipping a cocktail in the cool evening air, the views and atmosphere here will leave you mesmerized.

Unwind at this gorgeous open deck by the pool, with crisp fresh air, delicious food, and a starry night sky. The Terrace is an experience that goes beyond dining, its about enjoying the perfect combination of gourmet food, stunning views, and a serene atmosphere.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available at Araiya Palampur?

At Araiya Palampur, we offer a variety of dining options to suit every preference. Enjoy a delicious buffet breakfast and à la carte menu at The Dining Room, or indulge in a luxury dining experience at our The Terrace with stunning mountain views. For a more laid-back experience, visit The Old Fox Bar, where you can enjoy a drink while overlooking the pool and the hills. Whether you are in the mood for a quick snack or a gourmet meal, we have something for everyone.

Can I enjoy local cuisine at Araiya Palampur?

Yes! At Araiya Palampur, we take pride in offering authentic Kangri cuisine that reflects the local flavors of the region. Whether you are enjoying a meal at The Dining Room or a more casual dining experience at The Terrace, you can savor the rich, traditional tastes of Palampur, beautifully prepared by our expert chefs.

Does Araiya Palampur offer vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer a wide variety of vegetarian dishes at Araiya Palampur, from traditional local vegetarian fare to international vegetarian options. Whether you are dining at The Dining Room, enjoying a drink at The Old Fox Bar, or having a meal outdoors at The Terrace, our menu is designed to accommodate all dietary preferences.

Can I have a private dinner at Araiya Palampur?

Yes, we offer exclusive options for private dinners at Araiya Palampur. Whether you are looking for a romantic dinner at The Terrace with stunning views or a cozy private meal at The Dining Room, we can arrange a personalized dining experience to make your evening truly special.

Does Araiya Palampur offer outdoor dining?

Yes, Araiya Palampur offers an unforgettable outdoor dining experience at The Terrace. Set in a breathtaking location with views of the Dhauladhar range, this open-air dining option provides the perfect setting for an intimate dinner or a family gathering. Relax under the stars and enjoy gourmet food while surrounded by the natural beauty of Palampur.

What type of drinks are available at The Old Fox Bar?

At The Old Fox Bar, we offer a variety of drinks to suit every taste. From classic cocktails and premium wines to locally-inspired drinks, our bar has something for every guest. Whether you are in the mood for a relaxing drink overlooking the pool or enjoying a lively evening with friends, The Old Fox Bar is the perfect place to unwind.

Can I reserve a table for dining at Araiya Palampur?

Yes, you can easily reserve a table at Araiya Palampur for any of our dining experiences, whether its at The Dining Room, The Terrace, or The Old Fox Bar. We highly recommend making a reservation in advance, especially during peak seasons, to ensure availability.

Is there a dress code for dining at Araiya Palampur?

At Araiya Palampur, we encourage our guests to dress casually yet elegantly for all dining experiences. For dinner at The Terrace or The Dining Room, we recommend smart casual attire. The Old Fox Bar is more relaxed but still provides a sophisticated atmosphere for guests looking to unwind with drinks.

What makes the dining experience at Araiya Palampur special?

The dining experience at Araiya Palampur goes beyond just great food. Our luxury dining options, such as The Terrace with panoramic views of the Dhauladhar range, provide a setting like no other. Whether you are dining at The Dining Room or enjoying a drink at The Old Fox Bar, we offer an immersive gourmet experience that will delight all your senses.
