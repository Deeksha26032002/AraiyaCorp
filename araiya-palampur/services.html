With our roots firmly in the finest of hospitality management, we are very aware of our responsibility to the guest and the industry. The Araiya team comes with a passion to understand the modern hotel industry with careful attention to detail and constantly adapt to new trends.

Our expertise in hotel management and operations, attention to adaptive designs and technology-inclusive approach, innovative sales, and marketing strategies are efforts towards presenting a carefully thought of bouquet of services. At Araiya, our approach is a customer-driven one reflecting the best of Indian hospitality and global industry trends.

Hotel Development

At the heart of the Araiya advantage is the desire to provide the best value to our partners. Our personalised approach to development strategies, commitment to long-term and fruitful collaborations are targeted towards addressing specific needs of the owner and the project.

Our co-branding option assists owners to further their own brand exposure while our transparent and honest communications result in healthier synergies and higher returns on investments.

We develop and manage properties with lasting economic benefits. The unique Araiya touch is all about delivering the best experience to both guests and stakeholders.

Design & Construction

With Araiya, owners will find immense value thanks to our functional and tasteful designs aimed at providing superior experiences. Be it providing high value for guests and key stakeholders or being environmentally conscious, we remain motivated.

Optimum asset utilisation is key to Araiya. Our designs make our hotels cost-effective to build and manage, and we ensure maximum space efficiency.

While being committed to providing quality design and construction guidelines, we are always ready to go the extra mile to ensure luxury, comfort, and security for guests.

Hotel Operations

The modern-day traveller is constantly evolving and is unique, informed and connected. We are constantly adapting to the ever-changing trends to deliver better experiences.

Our operating ratios are lean and we ensure the most efficient use of resources while keeping our services engaging and personalised.

We emphasise on technology to build healthier relationships with our guests and make the Araiya experience richer via means of our feedback systems, our ORM and our mobile app.

Combining intuition with emotional quotient, we bring the warmth, intimacy, and character to hotel operations.

Sales & Marketing

Our team’s rich experience in the sector allows us to be confident about a profitable future. We are building on a solid foundation with robust teams dedicated to every segment – leisure, corporate and MICE. Our focus on low distribution costs further promises to drive revenue.

Araiya embraces the digital age providing guests booking access to our hotels that is direct and customised. With online technology, e-commerce, loyalty programmes, and effective sales database management, we are well-poised to achieve customer delight, repeat clientele, brand loyalty, and higher returns.
