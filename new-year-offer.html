New Year Package

    Package Includes

  • Welcome drink on arrival at Araiya Palampur – choose between an immunity booster drink and warming cocktails
  • Cookies and chocolate platter in room upon arrival
  • Stay in well-appointed luxurious rooms / suites


    • Your calendar for 30 December 2020 includes:

  • Afternoon tea at The Araiya Garden from 4 PM to 5 PM.
    Relish a variety of locally influenced finger snacks accompanied by our selection of fine leaf teas and Araiya blend coffee.
  • Noon at leisure.
    Prebook an activity from The Araiya Experiences or relax and indulge at the Araiya Spa (both at an additional cost).
  • A soothing evening with the best of regional and Indie tunes brought to you live by an immensely talented musical trio followed by an elaborate buffet dinner at The Dining Room from 8 PM to 10:30 PM.

  • Enjoy a buffet breakfast at The Dining Room
  • Indulge in the last afternoon tea of the year at The Araiya Garden from 4 PM to 5 PM. Enjoy a variety of locally influenced finger snacks accompanied by our selection of fine leaf teas and Araiya blend coffee
  • Bring in the new year to the tunes of an Indie quartet and sprawling views of the Dhauladhars at 5000ft up in the skies. This gala promises you an open bar (serving imported and IMFL spirits), abundant cocktail snacks, a buffet dinner, a DJ and 4 musicians to keep your spirits high.
    Where – The Terrace
    When – 8.30PM onwards
  • Extensive NYE buffet dinner with an eclectic mix of delicacies from local and global cuisines:
    Where – The Dining Room
    When – From 10 PM to 1 AM
  • Aerial firework for the countdown
  • Curated mementos at turndown for takeaway


    • 1 January 2021 – New Years’ Day

  • Continental à la carte breakfast will be available for the early risers at The Dining Room from 7 AM to 10 AM
  • Elaborate New Year’s Day Brunch at The Dining Room from 10:30 AM to 3 PM with the best of regional and Indie tunes brought to you live by an Indie band
