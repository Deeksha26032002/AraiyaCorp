Araiya Discovery

We are proud to be a member of the Global Hotel Alliance. Inspired by the belief that guests should have more choice alongside personalised reward, in 2004, GHA was born. Today, over 550 member hotels from all corners of the earth form GHA, making it the largest grouping of independent hotel brands in the world- including us. And we all share one mission.

We believe our membership to the Global Hotel Alliance makes us stronger. Through sharing our properties and their availability with GHA, we can reach discerning travellers all over the world, as we continue to grow, improve and enhance our guests’ experiences with us.
