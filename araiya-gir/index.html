    Signature Amenities at Araiya Gir

    POOL

    Dive into serenity at our stunning pool, designed for relaxation and leisure. Surrounded by lush landscapes, it's the perfect spot to unwind and soak up the sun. Enjoy gentle breezes and refreshing dips in a tranquil setting.

    ALL DAY DINING

    Savour delightful culinary creations at our all-day dining venue, featuring 57 indoor seats and 12 outdoor seats for an al fresco experience. Enjoy a diverse menu that caters to every palate, served in a welcoming and elegant atmosphere.

    OUTDOOR PRIVATE DINING

    Indulge in the ultimate dining experience with our outdoor private dining option, where intimacy meets the beauty of nature. Enjoy meals crafted with care while surrounded by the serene sounds of the outdoors. Perfect for special occasions, romantic dinners, or intimate gatherings.

    LAWNS AND ENTERTAINMENT CORNERS

    Explore our beautifully landscaped 4 lawns and multiple entertainment niches, perfect for gatherings and leisurely strolls. Each space is thoughtfully designed for relaxation and enjoyment, offering the ideal backdrop for celebrations, picnics, or quiet reflection. Embrace the great outdoors and discover the magic of nature at Araiya Gir.

    YOGA PAVILION

    Find your inner peace in our dedicated yoga pavilion, designed for relaxation and mindfulness. Surrounded by the beauty of nature, the pavilion offers a tranquil space for yoga sessions led by expert instructors. Embrace wellness and rejuvenation, enhancing your connection to both body and spirit amidst the serene landscape.
