How to Reach

By Air:

Keshod Airport: The nearest airport to Gir National Park, located approximately 70 km away. From Keshod, you can easily hire a cab or take a bus to reach the park.

Rajkot Airport: Situated about 160 km from Gir National Park, Rajkot Airport serves as another convenient entry point. Transportation options include taxis and buses for your journey to the park.

By Train:

Railway Stations: The primary railway hubs connecting to Gir National Park are Junagadh and Veraval. Both stations provide convenient access, making it easy to reach the park.

By Road:

Bus Services: Regular bus services operate from Junagadh and Veraval to Gir National Park, ensuring smooth travel for visitors.

Driving: For those opting to drive, Gir National Park is approximately 380 km from Ahmedabad, with an estimated travel time of around 7.5 hours.
