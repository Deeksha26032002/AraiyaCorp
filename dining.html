Dining

Enjoy gourmet dining in Palampur when you stay with us at the Araiya Palampur. As if the expansive menu is not enough, dining at the Araiya Palampur is a fine dining experience in every sense. Going beyond the food and drinks we serve; we offer a variety of gourmet dining experiences that will not just thrill your taste buds but excite your senses too.

Choose from the delicious and authentic local cuisine or the all-day dining menu and make every meal of yours a delightful episode in your break in the hills. Fancy fine dining with a view, a quiet beverage ata charming bar, or even fun picnics in the outdoors? We have them all for you here. All you need is your appetite.

The Dining Room

All Day Dining

A scrumptious buffet breakfast and an à la carte menu for lunch and dinner, you are spoilt for options here. You can opt for global gourmet dining or relish the local Kangri fare. The Dining Room has been tastefully designed so you can enjoy the meal of your choice to the fullest in a fine dining atmosphere.

Treat yourself to a gourmet dining experience that will linger on your taste buds and in your memory. All this in a fine dining setting that befits the luxurious experience you are promised with every moment you spend here.

Whether you are enjoying the first meal of the day, a lazy lunch, a quick snack or a relaxed, cosy dinner at the end of an eventful day, this all-day dining area throws open its doors and the choicest options for you.

The Old Fox

Bar

Step into The Old Fox Bar and let time move at just the leisurely pace you would like it to. Grab yourself a drink of your choice and relax in this tranquil lounge overlooking the pool and the mountains.

Luxurious interiors, sophisticated furnishing and a bar that would be the envy of many, this quaint spot in the resort is where you will want to be at on a laid-back afternoon or a beautiful evening. Take in the splendid views that surround you, watch the sun go down, relax with a good book in one hand and a drink in another, and enjoy a laid-back gourmet dining experience

What was it that Athenaeus said again? Old wood best to burn, old wine to drink, old friends to trust, and old authors to read . We are adding The Old Fox Bar to the mix.

The Terrace

Outdoor dining

With a magnificent view of the mountains and the valley, the outdoor dining space that is The Terrace is going to add an otherworldly charm tough to capture in words. Be it a private dinner or a get together of family and friends, this fine dining experience under the open skies is going to be hard to beat.

Unwind at this gorgeous open deck by the pool, as you overlook stunning views of the Dhauladhar range and the mountains that surround Palampur. Crisp, fresh air, delicious gourmet dining, and a starry, starry night sky, are just the tonic you need to put you in the holiday mood!
