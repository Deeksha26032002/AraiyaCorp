Araiya Enhanced Stays

Nothing is more important to us than the health and wellbeing of our guests and artisans alike. Rest assured, we have upgraded our standard hygiene practices to maintain a safe environment for your next getaway.

ARAIYA ENHANCED #SAFESTAYS

What to expect when you visit our resort

  • Contactless

    Payments, receipts, delivery, check-in and check-out.

  • Enhanced Sanitization

    Commonly used and high touch surfaces such as door handles, coffee tables, phones, switches, washbasin faucets, etc.

  • UV Lights

    Ultraviolet light will be employed for all items which cannot be sanitized such as car keys, mobile phones and luggage.

  • Social Distancing

    Furniture arrangement in our dining and all other common areas has been revised to ensure sufficient social distancing.

  • Food Safety

    Our artisans have been trained on safe food preparation and hygienic service practices. Regular audits will be conducted to ensure all safety standards are strictly complied to.
