Araiya Waterfront Serviced Apartments - Alibaug

Araiya Waterfront Serviced Apartments, Alibaug
As you stand at the Gateway of India and look across the bay, a new icon is about to emerge. Zip across the Bombay harbour to the Mandwa pier. A ten minute drive brings you to the latest landmark, the best luxury serviced apartments in Alibaug — Araiya Waterfront Serviced Apartments by Earthweavers.

A short drive through mango orchards and coconut groves, leads you to Dhokawade, a quaint neighbourhood centred around a water body and Goteshwar Mandir, a local shrine. As you enter the gates of the property, the gorgeous landscaping creates an ideal ambience for family and friends weekend getaways or corporate retreats. Drive through beautiful grounds until you arrive at the entrance. At thirteen stories tall, it promises to be a striking icon, the very first high rise on this side of the bay. As you enter the lobby on the ground floor, the Araiya team welcomes you.

There are only two apartments at each level, offering a sense of privacy and exclusivity. In keeping with our promise to bring you the best apartment hotel in Alibaug, Araiya’s security team works around the clock to ensure that you are safe and comfortable at all times.

These luxury serviced apartments in Alibaug offer an enormous sense of space and place. Each unit is 2100 square feet and opens out onto views of the serene waterbody. Chic and sleek, the interiors are equipped with everything you need to make it the best apartment hotel in Alibaug, ideal for family weekend getaways and corporate retreats. Individually controlled central air-conditioning, ceiling fans and cross-ventilation serve to ensure comfortable temperatures and humidity levels at all times, backed up by a generator incase the power fails, making your stay at the best serviced apartments in Alibaug a seamless experience. Spacious bedrooms offer residents our signature Araiya Bed Experience. Elegant bathrooms include a walk-in shower, as well as consciously sourced toiletries that are environmentally-friendly. Each luxury serviced apartment includes a washer + dryer and iron + ironing board, while the kitchenette has a refrigerator, a cooking range, dishwasher and all the appliances that you might need for a comfortable family and friends weekend getaway or corporate stay at Alibaug.

Sip on a cup of tea or coffee on your private deck-balcony, overlooking peaceful environs. Sunlight and birdsong filters through the leaves and in through large windows, equipped with screens to keep the insects out, to begin your day on a high note.

Feeling energetic? Head over to our well-equipped gym, staffed by Araiya trainers, take a stroll through the grounds or a dip in the pool to cool off. Our all-day bistro offers a delicious breakfast, made according to your preferences by the Araiya chefs.

Chill out and unwind? Relax in our spacious and comfortable lounge and simply curl up with a good book or a movie. As the day progresses, wander down to the Araiya Spa. Pamper yourself with a massage at the spa. Soak up a spectacular sunset from the rooftop terrace or raise a toast with a cocktail at the bar, before heading to the bistro for dinner. The Araiya team is always at hand to ensure that you have a seamless holiday experience in your luxury serviced apartment in Alibaug.

Need to plug in? Comfortable furnishings and technology make working from here a pleasure. Secure high-speed internet is available in each apartment, along with universal plug points for all your devices. The region also has mobile network connectivity via a range of service providers, and our in-house concierge can assist you, making it the best corporate serviced apartment in Alibaug

The Araiya Waterfront Serviced Apartments by Earthweavers, offers the best serviced apartments in Alibaug. It is just a short hop across the water from South Mumbai, by passenger ferry, speed boat or the Ro-Ro, and ample parking for residents and their guests. Whether you’re coming in for the weekend or staying a while longer, Araiya Waterfront brings you the perfect blend of luxury serviced apartments in Alibaug within a natural setting, a stone’s throw away from South Mumbai.

Earthweavers integrates traditional techniques, materials and forms, and interprets them into contemporary context, seamlessly blended with Araiya’s emphasis on personalized service and distinctive experiences. Fully staffed and managed by the Araiya team, this property promises to be your home away from home, without the hassle of taking care of it. Together, we offer smart, convenient and holistic hospitality, keeping the modern-day nomad in mind.

Fresh air, natural light, a delightful wind from the sea, and easy proximity to Mumbai, make a holiday or a corporate stay at Alibaug at the Araiya Waterfront Serviced Apartments by Earthweavers, a total breeze!
