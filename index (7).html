our Story

We understand hospitality in its truest sense. We are committed to the idea of providing personalised service that lead to distinctive experiences with Araiya. With designs and industry innovations that have been thought out keeping the modern-day traveller in mind,

ethos

The essence of who we are, what we want to do and how we plan to provide an experience like no other, is rooted in our name – Araiya. The word simply means ‘something beautiful and divine’.

the araiya signature

Emphasis on seamless technology, engaged team members marked by adaptive designs, the Araiya signature is one of the highest virtues. We are inspired by authenticity.

Leadership

Given our team’s past experience in launching hotels and resorts in India, Middle East, South East Asia,Europe and the Caribbean, combined with the strength of our marketing platform, we are confident of creating a unique, new concept to suit the location and market dynamics of the hotel.

CSR

At Araiya, we care. We understand we have a responsibility towards everything that helps us succeed and we are committed to give back more than what we take. We engage actively with the local community sourcing talent and the resources we need in the various departments in our properties.

services
We develop and manage properties with lasting economic benefits. The unique Araiya touch is all about delivering the best experience to both guests and stakeholders.
hotel development
Luxurious stays or comfort in value, we spare no effort to ensure our hotels are designed carefully to meet every need.
Design & Construction
With a deep understanding of guests and the hotel industry, we offer creative expertise in successful operations of properties.
Hotel Operations
Experts in all facets of sales in the hospitality industry, our sales force is dedicated to the growth of Araiya and provide value to the stakeholders’ interest and investment.
